Biography

Paolo Maldini was born on June 26, 1968, in Milan, Italy. He is the son of Cesare Maldini, a former AC Milan captain and Italian international. Paolo followed in his father's footsteps, joining AC Milan's youth academy at a young age.

He made his professional debut for AC Milan in 1985 at the age of 16 and quickly established himself as a key player. Known for his defensive prowess, leadership, and sportsmanship, Maldini became a symbol of loyalty and excellence in football.

Throughout his 25-year career, he remained with AC Milan, becoming the club's all-time appearance leader and winning numerous domestic and international titles.
