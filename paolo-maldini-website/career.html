Career

Paolo Maldini's professional career spanned 25 seasons, all spent at AC Milan. He made his debut in 1985 and quickly became a cornerstone of the team's defense.

He was known for his versatility, playing primarily as a left-back but also excelling as a central defender. Maldini captained AC Milan for over a decade, leading the team to numerous domestic and international titles.

His leadership on and off the pitch earned him respect worldwide, and he remains one of the most decorated players in football history.
